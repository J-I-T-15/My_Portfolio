Project Overview

The restaurant webpage, designed solely on the frontend, offers an engaging and visually appealing online experience for its visitors.

The homepage greets visitors with stunning high-resolution images of the restaurant's delectable dishes and inviting ambiance. The navigation menu at the top allows users to easily explore various sections, such as "Home", "Menu","About", and "Order".

The "Menu" section showcases the restaurant's offerings, organized into categories like appetizers, main courses, desserts, and beverages.
